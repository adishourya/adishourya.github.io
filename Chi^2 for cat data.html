CHI SQR STATISTIC FOR HYPO TESTING OR GOODNESS OF FIT

suppose in a quiz there are 4 options a , b ,c and d . Some students claim that the dist of correct answers were not uniform . so an independent group performs an expirement by randomly sampling 100 questions from the given question bank and gets the result as shown below

option expected actual
a 25 20
b 25 20
C 25 25
d 25 35

let α = 5% H₀ : p(a|b|c|d) = 0.25 H₁ : p(a|b|c|d) ≠ 0.25

so χ² is calculated as Σ (expected - actual)² / expected

χ² = (25/25) + (25/25) + 0 + (100/25) = 6 df = ε -1 = 4-1 =3 


In [1]: import scipy.stats as st

In [2]: st.chi2.sf(6,df=3)
Out[2]: 0.11161022509471268

since the pvalue = 11.16 % is > sig. level 5% There is not enough evidence to reject null hypothesis.
