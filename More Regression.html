ANOVA

  • Anova is a statistical technique that is used to check if the means of 2 or more group are significantly different from one another
  • Anova checks the impact of one or more factors by comparing means of different samples
  • Another measure to compare the samples is called a t-test
  • when we have only 2 samples t-test and anova are the same thing
