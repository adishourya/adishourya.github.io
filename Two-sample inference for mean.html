Constructing t-interval for diff of means

Like in diff of prop diff of mean would be done as

(x_bar1 - x_bar2) ± t* σ₁₂ now dev would be calculated as expected sample dev of sample / √n

and t* would be determined by significance level dof for t* would be min (n₁ - 1 , n₂ - 1)

Example

kyle suspects that when people exercise more their body temp changes group1 exercised for 30 mins : n1 = 18 ; x_bar1 = 38.3 ; s1 = 0.27 group2 exercised for 60 mins : n2 = 24 ; x_bar1 = 38.9 ; s1 = 0.29
