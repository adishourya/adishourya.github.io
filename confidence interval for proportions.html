Intro

  • There is a 95% chance that P (true pop proportion) lies within 2σ_phat of p_hat
    • where p_hat is the sample proportion of proportion & σ_phat = √pq/n
    • for a 95% confidence level/interval

Standard Error

is just σ_phat

Conditions for valid c.i

  1. should be a truly random sample
  2. Assuming sample dist of sample prop is normal :: shape of sample dist.

There are 2 rules of thumb to see if sample dist of sample prop is normal

  • np ≥ 10
  • nq ≥ 10
  • Independence rule. i.e sampling with replacement can be assumed if sample size ≤ 10% of pop size
