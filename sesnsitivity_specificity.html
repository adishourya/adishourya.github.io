A confusion matrix for 2 outcomes looks as such

  Actual Positive Actual Negative

Predicted Negative FN TN

Once the confusion matrix is filled out we can calculate 2 useful matrix sensitivity and specificity

Sensitivity
Tells us what percentage of patients with heart disease were
correctly classified . (i.e the first col.)

Sensitivity = True Positive / Actual Positive ∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙

Specificity
Tells us what percentage of patients without heart disease
were correctly classified

Specificity
True Negative / Actual Negative
