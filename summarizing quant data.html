IQR

Basically
diff b/n the first half and the second half
  • suppose sample: 1,3,2,4,6,5,7,9,8
  • then arrange them : 1,2,3,4,5,6,7,8,9
  • so we got 4 nos on either side of 5 :: median
  • first half median 7.5
  • second half median 2.5
  • IQR = 7.5 - 2.5 = 5
