Nothing much to see here There is a tradeoff between bias and variance checkout : bias_variance

as the degree of the curve fit increases variance increases and bias decreases as the degree of the curve fit decreases variance decreases and bias increases

for .eg since a straight line would fail to be best fit as it would have low variance (low degree) and high bias (not all the points could pass through)

There are some commonly used methods for finding the sweet spot like regularization , boosting and bagging
